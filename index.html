Adapt Your Tool to Your Business in Minutes, No Developer Required

Are you bored of switching between multiple admin interfaces to get your job done? Are you fed up of being dependent of the IT guys to update all the information you need on your dashboard? Have you already dreamt of a simple and usable interface to help with your daily tasks? Give a try to misocell!

misocell gathers your data from one or several datasources, aggregating them into simple and easy-to-use dashboards and forms. Empower your business in a few clicks, without IT support, and produce eye-candy powerful screens.

Rearrange Information For Your Own Needs

Your work is not exactly the same than your colleagues. You need different data for your own tasks. With misocell, you can customize all your display to fulfill your exact requirements. And no need of compromise, each member of your team has its own interface.

Adding columns, re-ordering form fields, removing unsignificant decimals or hide some meaningless data is a simple and efficient process. In a few clicks, edit your screens and get a real-time rendering.

Advanced Data Visualization

Big list of numbers are hard to read and cumbersome to interpret. Generally, we export the data into a spreadsheet software and use its advanced chart features. That's just work. But at each update, your file is obsolete, and you need to re-import them.

misocell assists you in turning your data into nice interactive visualizations directly into your browser. No more data desynchronization: misocell works on the original and fresh data. And, icing on the cake, you can embed these charts into your next meeting slideshows, as you did before.

Plug to Public API with Ease

You probably use some open-source softwares or third-part applications. Social networks, Content Management Systems (CMS), or Entreprise Resource Planning (ERP) softwares... Mixing data between all these datasources is often synonym of a long and tiresome export/import dance, including an important error factor and some outdated data probability.

misocell already supports a wide range of datasources out of the box. Plug your Customer Relationship Manager (CRM) on your customer Twitter feeds to get their feedback in real-time, link your Events task to MeetUp to share instantaneously your updates with your community, or publish a new post on your WordPress blog once a new feature planned on Trello has been released!

Perform Your Activities From Everywhere

Unleash your team potential providing them a way to accomplish their task far from the office, directly on their mobile devices: cell phones or tablets, Android or iPhones, in network covered areas or even in offline mode!

misocell provides you a privilegied interface to your datasources, helping you assuming your tasks wherever you are, with the same usability and features than the desktop version. No installation required, just connect to misocell, and you're ready to go!
